About us

We are mO=On, a Conrad Challenge 2024-2025 team of four from Oxford High School.
Our mission is to provide a service to efficiently and safely transport and store oxygen once it has been extracted from the moon's surface. This oxygen can then be sold to space agencies to use for a variety of purposes to aid moon exploration.
We envision our innovative solution to be able to provide a reliable and sustainable oxygen supply, laying the foundations for a future successful moon base, and solve the problem where lots of money is being spent to transport huge amounts of oxygen to the moon.
Sustainability is one of our main core values, so we also aim to find a way to create an oxygen cycle on the moon, making this process sustainable and minimising any permanent harm done to the moon.

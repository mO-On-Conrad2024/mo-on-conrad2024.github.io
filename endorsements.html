Endorsements

Every kind word and thoughtful insight we have received is a step forward in our mission. We are really proud to share some of the support that has helped shape mO=On’s journey.


"Huge congratulations to the four students in the mO=On team from Oxford High School GDST, who have been selected as finalists of the Conrad Challenge, a global innovation competition creating the next generation of entrepreneurs who will change the world! The mO=On team reached the finals based on their idea to provide a sustainable service to extract, store and transport liquid oxygen and hydrogen to customers on the Moon. They aim to provide gases to fuel trips from the Moon to Mars, and to enable humans to live in lunar settlements.
Surrey Space Centre's expert on lunar construction and in-situ resource utilisation, Dr Sungwoo Lim, was one of the team's advisers, but he gives all credit to the student team. They'll be heading to the 2025 Innovation Summit which takes place over four days at Space Center Houston and NASA's Johnson Space Center next month. We think they're going to have a fantastic time!"

- Surrey Space Centre

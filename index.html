The Breath of Space Exploration


We are mO=On, and we present a solution to extracting oxygen on the moon, providing a reliable supply of oxygen to our customers while keeping sustainability in mind.
What makes us unique is that we combine previous research with our own, using preexisting extraction technology and adding on a storage system. The whole process is automated, maximising efficiency. We are hopeful our solution would be able to assist space agencies in achieving space exploration goals.

About us

We are a Conrad Challenge 2024-2025 team of four from Oxford High School, and all of us play an important role in making this a successful project.

Our product

We sell canisters of oxygen to our customers, which have been extracted from the lunar surface, stored and constantly monitored.
